My Biography Professor Jerry Obi-Okogbuo FCAI, KSJI, ULULO, B.Phil.Hons.(Uban); M.A., Ph.D.(UNN). Professor Jerry Obi Okogbuo is a Professor of Philosophy (Philosophy of Science). He is currently the Director, Directorate of General Studies, Federal University of Technology, Owerri, Imo State. He was on a Sabbatical at the Institute of Management and Technology, IMT, Enugu, 2011 - 2016 during which he functioned as the Executive Assistant, EA, to the Rector, Institute of Management and Technology, IMT, Enugu. Professor Jerry Obi Okogbuo was the Deputy Director, Academic Planning and Development, AP&D, FUT, Owerri, 2006 - 2010. Professor Jerry Obi Okogbuo is an Adjunct Professor at the Seat of Wisdom Seminary, Owerri, Imo State. He is a member of the Seminary Central Appraisal Committee (SCAC Academic) and Appointment and Promotions Committee (A&PC Academic).
